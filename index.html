Chalé Hotel - Experience the Charm of Brazil



At Chalé Hotel, we welcome you to indulge in the serene beauty and warm hospitality of our accommodations across various enchanting locations in Brazil – Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, and Belo Horizonte.

Each of our charming chalés (chalets) offers a unique blend of comfort, elegance, and Brazilian flair, promising an unforgettable stay in the heart of this diverse and vibrant country.

At Chalé Hotel, we strive to create a memorable and authentic Brazilian experience for our guests, whether you're seeking adventure, relaxation, or cultural immersion.

Come and discover the beauty and hospitality of Brazil with us at Chalé Hotel !!!

  • Rio de Janeiro:

    Nestled amidst the breathtaking landscapes of Rio de Janeiro, our Chalé Hotel offers a picturesque retreat surrounded by lush greenery and stunning vistas. Whether you're exploring the iconic landmarks like Christ the Redeemer and Sugarloaf Mountain or simply relaxing on the pristine beaches of Copacabana and Ipanema, our cozy chalés provide the perfect sanctuary after a day of adventure.

  • São Paulo:

    In the bustling metropolis of São Paulo, our Chalé Hotel provides a tranquil oasis away from the hustle and bustle of the city. Located amidst the vibrant cultural scene and bustling business district, our chalés offer a peaceful haven where you can unwind and recharge. Explore the city's renowned museums, art galleries, and gastronomic delights before retiring to the comfort of your own private chalet.

  • Belo Horizonte:

    Set against the backdrop of Belo Horizonte's charming colonial architecture and scenic landscapes, our Chalé Hotel invites you to experience the rich cultural heritage of Minas Gerais. From exploring the historic squares and vibrant markets to sampling the local cuisine and nightlife, there's no shortage of experiences to discover in this dynamic city. After a day of exploration, retreat to your cozy chalet and savor the tranquility of our lush garden surroundings.
